About the Lab

KB-Infinity Lab is an applied research laboratory focused on bridging advanced academic research with deployable, resource-efficient autonomous and intelligent systems. The lab integrates robotics, Model Predictive Control (MPC), machine learning, embedded systems, and cybersecurity.

The lab is aligned with PhD-level research and operates in close connection with academic institutions, industry partners, and innovation ecosystems.

Research Pillars

Autonomous Systems

Autonomous Ground Vehicles (AGVs), mobile robots, navigation and planning.

Intelligent Control

Model Predictive Control (MPC), hybrid MPC + learning, robust control.

Edge AI & Perception

Computer vision, deep learning, efficient inference on embedded platforms.

Cyber-Physical Security

AI-based intrusion detection and anomaly monitoring for IoT systems.

Flagship Projects

URBAN-NAV

Uncertainty-aware vision-based autonomous navigation using hybrid MPC on edge robotics platforms.

SAFE-EDGE

AI-driven anomaly detection and safety monitoring for autonomous and cyber-physical systems.

AI-Based IDS for IoT

Hybrid CNN–Transformer–BiLSTM intrusion detection system optimized for resource-constrained environments.

Intelligent MPC for AGVs

Real-time trajectory planning, obstacle avoidance, and adaptive control for autonomous ground vehicles.
